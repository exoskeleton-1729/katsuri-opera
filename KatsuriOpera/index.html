About the Opera

La Racconta de' Katsuri (The Katsurou Saga) tells the story of Enigma, the one true goddess of the universe who decides to adopt a daughter, Cyto, to ease her loneliness. However, Cyto falls in love with a human gangster, Jimmy, and the couple eventually adopts a child, Scythe. Little do they know that Scythe is not what she seems. The story is taken from "The Katsurou Saga", written by Anika Iyer, Matthew Nguyen, Alison Shaw, and Karen Wu, and was adapted into a libretto by Matthew Nguyen. The score was composed by Elliot Lichtman, and the verses by Matthew Nguyen, with guidance from Anika Iyer.
